Donations

    On this page you can find the organizations we value and help support them with donations! We swim to raise awareness for these organizations and foster connections with the swim community and our oceans.
  1. Swim Across America
    2. We swim with the SwimAcrossAmerica organization every year and love participating in the fun swims in the San Francisco Bay making waves to fight cancer!
  2. The Ocean Cleanup
    3. The Ocean cleanup is helping rid the oceans of harmful plastics and aims to clean up 90% of floating ocean plastic pollution by 2040.
  3. Marine Mammal Center
    4. We love seeing George the seal on our weekly excursions swimming in the bay! The Marine Mammal Center helps rehabilitate seals and sea lions in the Bay Area and we would love your support helping our swim buddies.
